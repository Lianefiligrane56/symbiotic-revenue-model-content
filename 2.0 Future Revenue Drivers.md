### Key Design Take‑aways for a Sustainable SYM Tokenomics

1. **Balanced Revenue Mix** – Aim for ~60 % fee‑derived income, ~40 % inflationary rewards. Pure inflation erodes token value; pure fees can become volatile as TVL fluctuates.
2. **Dynamic Fee Tiers** – Lower fees for long‑term SYM stakers ([e.g](http://e.g/)., < 0.02 % on LST mint‑burn) to reward lock‑up and align incentives.
3. **Buy‑Back & Burn Loop** – Allocate 20‑30 % of net protocol revenue to auto‑buy SYM and burn, providing a price floor and counter‑inflation.
4. **Vesting & Anti‑Dump** – New SYM emissions for restakers vest over 12‑18 months with a linear release schedule, smoothing supply shocks.
5. **Risk‑Adjusted Yield** – Offer _boosted_ yields for SYM‑stakers who also provide first‑loss capital. This creates a clear risk‑reward ladder.
6. **Cross‑Chain Flexibility** – Design the token to be usable on multiple L1/L2 ecosystems (ERC‑20, native Cosmos, Solana SPL) so revenue streams can flow across networks without friction.

### Bottom Line

- **Restaking** is the engine; **fees, premiums, and smart‑token economics** are the fuel.
- A _hybrid_ model—mixing fee revenue with modest, TVL‑scaled inflation—creates the most resilient SYM economy.
- Embedding SYM utility (governance weight, fee discounts, boost rights, insurance capital) directly into each revenue stream aligns token holders with the protocol’s long‑term health.
